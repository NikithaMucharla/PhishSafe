Privacy policy

Information Collection
  • Collected Information:

    PhishSafe may collect and store names voluntarily provided by users for demonstration or educational purposes within the project.

  • No Other Personal Information:

    PhishSafe does not collect additional personally identifiable information (such as addresses or contact details).

Information Usage
  • Usage Data:

    PhishSafe may collect usage data for educational analysis and project evaluation.

  • Education and Analysis:

    The collected data, including names, is solely used for educational purposes within the scope of the college project. It may be analyzed to understand usage patterns, trends, and project effectiveness.

Data Security
  • Security Measures:

    Simulated user data, including names, is treated with care and stored securely within the project environment.

  • No Real-Time Protection:

    PhishSafe does not provide real-time phishing link protection or detection for actual users.

Cookies and Tracking
  • No Cookies or Tracking:

    PhishSafe does not utilize cookies or tracking technologies as it is a simulated project for educational purposes.

Children's Privacy
  • Not Directed to Children:

    PhishSafe is not directed towards individuals under the age of 13 as it is a college-level project.

Third-Party Links
  • No Third-Party Links:

    The project does not include any real or simulated third-party links or integrations.

Changes to Privacy Policy
  • Policy Scope:

    This privacy policy applies solely to the PhishSafe college project and may be updated for educational purposes or project enhancements without notice.

Contact Information

For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the PhishSafe college project, please contact phishhsafe@gmail.com
